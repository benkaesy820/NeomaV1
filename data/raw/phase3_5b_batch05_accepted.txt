This also stays safer when future code adds a new sensitive field to the object, because the logger does not include it automatically. Values that are still sensitive should be redacted or replaced with a non-secret identifier.

- Even allowlisted fields need length limits when they come from external input.

- Promotes safe defaults and change-resistant logging.
